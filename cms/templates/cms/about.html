Neurion was founded at the verge of the centuries and millenia, back in 2000

Technically, than it was the sole online blog about the popular science. Of course, if we count that in sense of a blog, that’s being regularly updated and has publications by worldwide renown scientists and researchers… Additionally, there was some inspiration behind this idea. Originally, we were incredibly inspired by the legacy that the “Scientific American” magazine had garnered throughout its 100+ years of the paperback circulation. We aspired to become an online exemplary of what this notable scientific magazine has been for the offline, printed scientific magazines…

So, we were eager to employ as many science experts, practicing scientists, university professors, think tanks leaders and researchers as only possible. All we can say, is that we’ve succeeded in that! As of this moment, more than 200 people yearly contribute to our articles either as writers or just an experts… With over 5000 articles, researches and op-eds already published, we lead the industry.

And to this day our goal remains to be the mission of carrying the torch of light and knowledge further. We’re sure that our efforts have already helped to popularize science for the next generation of Americans. We will carry on to work on for the next generations to come!
